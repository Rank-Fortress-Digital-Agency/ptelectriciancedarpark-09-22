What can Commercial Electricians Do


As explained earlier, commercial electricians handle the wiring and electrical systems of commercial settings. Part of their job is to maintain, repair, inspect or even upgrade the wiring system as per the requirement. Some of their duties are given below.

  1. Installing essential components such as switches, fuse boxes, wiring systems and similar elements of an electrical system
  2. Maintaining, repairing and upgrading the electrical systems according to the requirements.
  3. Inspecting all the wiring and electrical systems to ensure safety and adherence to the necessary protocols and guidelines.
  4. Extensive usage of technical drawings, including understanding and using them for doing their job
  5. Planning the robust electrical systems for a new construction
  6. Training new apprentices and leading teams of junior electricians (senior commercial electricians do it more frequently)
